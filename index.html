Project Overview

A digital transformation platform for clinics: taking everything from offline to online. We help clinics manage customers, doctors, appointments, services, and payments — all in one place!

What are we building?

This project delivers two websites:
- Frontend for clinic customers: book appointments, view services, find doctors, manage personal profiles.
- Backend for clinic staff/admins: manage bookings, patient records, revenue, and promote the clinic brand.
In short: We digitize all the workflow of a traditional offline clinic and bring it fully online.

Why does it matter?

- Empowers clinics to fully embrace digital, reaching more clients anytime, anywhere.
- Clients can book appointments in seconds — no more waiting in line.
- Streamlines management, cuts paperwork, reduces mistakes.
- Boosts business: higher revenue, better brand recognition, and smarter operations.

Who is it for?

1. Clients/Patients: Gen Z, Millennials, or anyone who wants fast and convenient online clinic services.
2. Clinic owners & staff: Admins, receptionists, doctors who use the backend to manage operations, serve clients, and grow their business.
3. Dev Team: A passionate group of PTIT computer engineering students, committed to real-world tech solutions.
